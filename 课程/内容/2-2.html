    每日天气

    麦克：今天天气真热！ Mike: It's so hot today!

    玛丽：是啊。也门夏天也很热吗？ Mary: Yes.Is summer in Yemen also hot?

    麦克：那儿比上海凉快。 Mike: It's cooler than Shanghai.

    玛丽：大概多少度？ Mary: What is the average temperature?

    麦克：最高二十几度。 Mike: The highest is a bit over 20 degrees.

    玛丽：那还好。Mary: That's not bad.

    词汇含义

    热: hot, warm

    夏天: summer

    凉快: cool,chilly

    度: degree (usually celsius)
