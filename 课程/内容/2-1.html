    时间表达法

    麦克：现在几点？ Mike: What time is it?

    玛丽：现在五点。 Mary: It's 5 o'clock.

    麦克：明天星期几？ Mike: What day will it be tomorrow??

    玛丽：明天星期一。 Mary: It will be Monday.

    麦克：你们从几点到几点上课？ Mike: What time do you have classes?

    玛丽：我从八点到11点上课。Mary: I have classes from 8 till 11 o'clock.

    词汇含义

    明天: tomorrow

    星期: day of the week

    有课: have classes

    上课: attend classes
