    用数字计数

    麦克：你家有几口人？ Mike: How many people are there in your family?

    玛丽：我家有四口人。 Mary: There are four people in my family

    麦克：你的生日是几月几号？ Mike: When is your birthday?

    玛丽：我的生日是2月3号。 Mary: My birthday is February 3rd.

    麦克：现在几点？ Mike: What time is it?

    玛丽：现在四点十分。Mary: It's ten past four now.

    词汇含义

    家: home/house

    人: people

    口 : measure word for people

    月: month

    日: day

    注意！

    口语的日期格式永远是是mm/dd，从来都不说dd/mm

    When speaking the layout of the date is always mm/dd，never dd/mm
