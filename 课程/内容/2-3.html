    想吃什么？

    麦克：你想吃包子还是饺子？ Mike: Do you want to eat steamed buns or dumplings?

    玛丽：我都不想吃。咱们吃面条吧。 Mary: Neither. Let's get noodles instead.

    麦克：我不想吃面条。我最近每天都吃面条。 Mike: I don't want to eat noodles.I've been eating noodles everyday recently.

    玛丽：那你想吃什么？ Mary :Well then what would you like to eat?

    麦克：吃披萨，怎么样？ Mike: How about pizza?

    玛丽：好主意。我们走吧。Mary: Good idea. Let's go!

    词汇含义

    包子: steamed buns

    饺子: dumpling

    面条: noodels

    主意: idea
